What is Aperture and how is this measured?
Aperture is measured by f-stops, which is the ratio between the focal length of the lens and the actual diameter diaphragm opening.



What is a Large Depth of Field?
A large Depth of Field is when the aperture f/16 or smaller.



How do you ensure your photo has a large Depth of field?
Make sure that the aperture is set to f/16 or smaller.



What is a Small Depth of Field?
A small depth of field is when the aperture is f/1.4-f/5.6.



How do you ensure your photo has a small Depth of Field?
Make sure the aperture is set to f/1.4-f/5.6
